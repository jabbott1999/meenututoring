Hello!
I'm Meenu.

I am a native, CELTA qualified (Grade A) teacher of English. I have been teaching English for almost ten years. I specialise in teaching EFL (English as a Foreign Language) to adults.

After graduating with an LL.B (Hons) Law degree, I started a career in advertising research. After several years of working in this industry, I decided to pursue a career in television. I worked as a researcher for documentaries and factual entertainment for various companies including the BBC and ITV.

I discovered teaching some years later and taught in private language schools in London for eight years. Last year, I finally decided to leave the rat race behind and start my own venture in what I do best...teaching!

I am an enthusiastic and friendly teacher and it gives me great satisfaction from seeing students achieve their goals. I am very passionate about teaching and I believe this is reflected in the positive feedback I have received throughout my training and employment.

SERVICES

  • General English: Beginner to Advanced

  • Exam preparation: IELTS & Cambridge exams (PET, FCE and CAE)
