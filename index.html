About Me

Hello! I'm Meenu.

I am a native, CELTA qualified (Grade A) teacher of English. I have been teaching English for almost ten years. I specialise in teaching EFL (English as a Foreign Language) to adults.

After graduating with an LL.B (Hons) Law degree, I started a career in advertising research. After several years of working in this industry, I decided to pursue a career in television. I worked as a researcher for documentaries and factual entertainment for various companies including the BBC and ITV.

I discovered teaching some years later and taught in private language schools in London for eight years. Last year, I finally decided to leave the rat race behind and start my own venture in what I do best...teaching!

I am an enthusiastic and friendly teacher and it gives me great satisfaction from seeing students achieve their goals. I am very passionate about teaching and I believe this is reflected in the positive feedback I have received throughout my training and employment.

How it Works

Free Assessment

I send each new student an online test to complete in order to assess their level of English followed by a phone conversation where I will conduct a short oral test. Thereafter, we will discuss any particular areas you wish to work on. I tailor my lessons to each student's specific needs.

Due to the recent Corona virus outbreak, all lessons will be held online. Once government restrictions are eased, face to face lessons will resume.

What is your cancellation policy?

Understandably, situations may arise where you are unable to attend a lesson. As such, in the event of cancellation, the following will apply:

At least 48 hours’ notice is required if you wish to cancel a lesson. This gives me the opportunity to reschedule and slot in other students where possible. If no notice is received or where less than 48 hours’ notice is given, the lesson will be charged at the full rate. In the event of illness or if circumstances have arisen which have not been foreseen, please let me know at the earliest opportunity possible.

In the event that I have to cancel the lesson, you will be given as much notice as possible and offered a different date or a full refund.
